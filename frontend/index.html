About Us

Our platform connects donors with charities, making it easier for charities to manage their profiles and for donors to find and support causes they care about.

How It Works

  • Charities can register and create detailed profiles.
  • Admins manage user and charity registrations, ensuring the platform's integrity.
  • Users can browse and donate to approved charities.
